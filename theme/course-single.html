About Shamiz Designs

Shamiz designs is a clothing brand that is being lead by a wonderful lady Suzy who is the sole owner. She started her Cloth line in 2009. Her small boutique later became a whole Fashion House and right now Shamiz Designs has merged with Ruggeye Collection, Tailor Micky and many other African Fashion house in creating one of the biggest fashion show in the entire Africa Continent.
Her main objective is to bring the Fashion World into live by her Crafty hands
