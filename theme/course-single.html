About Shamiz Designs

Shamiz designs is a clothing brand that is being lead by a wonderful lady Suzy who is the sole owner. She started her Cloth line in 2009. Her small boutique later became a whole Fashion House and right now, Shamiz Designs has merged with Rugeyye Collections, Tailor Micky and many other African Fashion houses in creating one of the biggest fashion show in the entire African Continent.
Her main objective is to bring the Fashion World to life by her Crafty hands
